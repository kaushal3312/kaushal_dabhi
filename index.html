Who am I :

Ever since I was a little kid, software development has been my passion.Born in Ahmedabad,Gujarat in 1995 I was fortunate enough to grow up with rapid changes in technology.I got to witness the end of some technologies and the advent of other technologies.The first computer language I learned was HTML - back when MySpace was a thing and you could customize profiles by embedding HTML. This happened at the age of 12. After buffing up a bit on HTML in high school, my first year of college unfolded and Java was the second computer language I learned.After then I learned C,C++, PHP, .NET tearing through any books I could find on anything remotely related to software development.The passion carried me through my education at Indus University in Computer Engineering.During my bachelors I got densely interested in Android SDK and rooting the mobile phones and playing with android ROMs and kernels. I also studied data structures, algorithms, compilers, computer architecture , higher mathematics, with same enthusiasm that drove me as kid.After completing my bachelors i did my internship at Sanctum Networks Ltd, Bengaluru for 3 months and after that I was working as Product Engineer there focusing mainly on Product Development, Python, Networking. And it was here that I learned truly how much there was yet to learn. And hence I decided to do my Master of science in Artificial Intelligence and Big Data in SRH University, Berlin.I've considered myself devoted to computer science and will always be. ❤
